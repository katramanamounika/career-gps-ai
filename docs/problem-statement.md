# Career GPS AI

## Problem

Students often struggle to identify suitable career paths, understand industry requirements, and build a structured roadmap toward employment.

Most students rely on fragmented resources such as YouTube videos, blogs, job portals, and social media, leading to confusion and inefficient career planning.

## Solution

Career GPS AI is an intelligent career navigation platform that analyzes a student's skills and profile to provide:

- Career Match Scores
- Skill Gap Analysis
- Personalized Learning Roadmaps
- Project Recommendations
- Career Readiness Tracking

## Target Users

- College Students
- Fresh Graduates
- Career Switchers

## Impact

Career GPS AI reduces career confusion and improves employability through personalized guidance.
